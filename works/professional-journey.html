Professional Journey: Ideation to Implementation

Professional Journey was an experimental and a very innovative project directed to enable users create content (their visual resume) on SlideShare in a few clicks. The user flow is designed in a such a simple way that you just need to connect your LinkedIn and SlideShare accounts, select a template and publish. You're done!

My role in this project was to create a landing page and an interactive playful template highlighting the important parts of the user's professional journey (from introduction to contact options, education to experience, key skills to recommendations).

Responsive Template

The professional journey template needed to cater to the following product and design objectives:

  • Out of the box and creative thinking,
  • Captures key information from the user's LinkedIn profile,
  • Interactive, fun and playful, engaging,
  • Fully responsive and supported on desktop and mobile devices, SlideShare's Slideview page and embeds.

My approach was to keep the design simple and minimal. I wanted to create a distraction free, uncluttered viewing experience and decided to highlight each career milestone one at a time in hierarchical order. I made use of vibrant colors and custom font combinations to make the design look alive and visually fresh. Last but not the least, I also added subtle animations and transitions to guide the user navigate through the professional journey easily.
